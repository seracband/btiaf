BIO


Download Hi-Res Press Photo

Serac is a post-noise metal band from Minneapolis, Minnesota. Their debut EP Waging War on the Prairie was released in 2017. A tar-laden 20-minute exercise in climate disaster which would lay the groundwork for releases to come.

Serac is comprised of guitarist Brent Ericson (Wanderer) guitarist Cole Benson (Livid[Prosthetic Records] Another Heaven) drummer Josh Dingle (ex-Aziza) and bassist/guitarist/keys Christian Moser (Another Heaven, ex-Former Worlds). Pulled from disparate parts of the heavy music scene in Minneapolis, Serac crafts a unique approach that forges varied influences into a cohesive sound.

Baring Teeth in a Famine builds on the lumbering low-end foundation of Waging War on the Prairie (2017) with a sound that is both expansive and urgent. Ranging from cinematic post-metal to discordant noise rock, death-metal inclinations and atmospheric key arrangements; the band manages to synthesize diverse influences into a style all their own.

Lyrically the album continues with the themes of ecological disaster and societal deterioration. Detailing a nameless protagonist navigating a fictional empire on the precipice of collapse; enduring the cultural hysterics that accompany the calamity.

Serac has opened for Big Business, Ken Mode, Birds in Row and other notable acts in heavy music. They are currently finishing the writing process for another EP and plan to record at the end of the year.
